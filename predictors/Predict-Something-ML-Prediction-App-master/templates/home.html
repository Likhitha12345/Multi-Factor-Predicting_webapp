Welcome to the home page of the app.

It shows the application and deployment of machine learning models on the given dataset and make us predict the entities of which the datasets are provided.

Overview:

Nowadays, everything around us are getting digital and the use of artificial intelligence, espacially, machine learning can be seen everywhere. So, in this app, the main motive is to train some machine learning models and try to deploy them for public use.

Applications:

  • To check the type of Glass.
  • To decrease the dependency on the doctors just for report checking.
  • To check the variety of flower.
  • To predict whether the forest is endangered or not.
  • For developers, how to deploy ML webapp using heroku.
